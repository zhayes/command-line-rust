About the Author

Ken Youens-Clark is a software developer, teacher, and writer. He began his undergraduate studies at the University of North Texas, initially with a focus in jazz studies (drums) and then changing his major several times before limping out of school with a BA in English literature. Ken learned coding on the job starting in the mid-1990s and has worked in industry, in research, and at nonprofits. In 2019, he earned his MS in biosystems engineering from the University of Arizona.
